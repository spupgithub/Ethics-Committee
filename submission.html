Procedures

  1. Log in to you MS Teams account
  2. Using MS Teams, upload your zipped folder containing the following files in your OneDrive (follow figure below):
    1. Application for Ethical Evaluation of Proposed Study -- Download here
    2. Thesis/Dissertation Adviser's Certification Form -- Download here
    3. Informed Consent Form -- Download here Download here
    4. Curriculum Vitae
    5. Payment Reciept for Ethics Review
    6. Minutes of Meeting
    7. Proposed Study Document
    8. Data Collection Tool/Questionnaire (if applicable)
  3. Share your zipped folder to mailto:cprint_ethicsreview@spup.edu.ph (follow figure below)

  4. You will receive an acknowledgement notification through MS Teams Chat.
  5. You will recieve an Approval request from cprint_ethicsreview@spup.edu.ph with the review result and the Ethics Compliance Matrix Form attached. Choose Approve (this means RECEIVED).
  6. Download the files and put them together in a folder. Accomplish the Ethics Compliance Matrix Form and the revision of the documents as per recommendation.
  7. Send an Approval request to cprint_ethicsreview@spup.edu.ph with the zipped folder attached. You will receive an approval notification. Choose Approve (this means RECEIVED).
  8. You receive an Approval request from cprint_ethicsreview@spup.edu.ph with your REC Certificate attached. Choose Approve (this means RECEIVED).
