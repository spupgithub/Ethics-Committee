Process of Submission

Hand-over

  1. Submit the following documents to Mr. Christian Jade Ruam in the CPRINT Office:
    1. Application for Ethical Evaluation of Proposed Study
    2. Thesis/Dissertation Adviser's Certification Form
    3. Informed Consent Form
    4. Curriculum Vitae
    5. Payment Reciept for Ethics Review
    6. Minutes of Meeting
    7. Proposed Study Document (Research Proposal)
    8. Data Collection Tool/Questionnaire (if applicable)

Online

  1. Log in to you MS Teams account
  2. Using MS Teams, upload your folder containing the following files in your OneDrive (follow figure below):
    1. Application for Ethical Evaluation of Proposed Study -- Download here
    2. Thesis/Dissertation Adviser's Certification Form -- Download here
    3. Informed Consent Form -- Download here Download here
    4. Curriculum Vitae
    5. Payment Reciept for Ethics Review
    6. Minutes of Meeting
    7. Proposed Study Document (Research Proposal)
    8. Data Collection Tool/Questionnaire (if applicable)
  3. Share your folder to cprint_ethicsreview@spup.edu.ph (follow figure below)

  4. You will receive an acknowledgement notification through MS Teams Chat.
  5. You will recieve an approval request from cprint_ethicsreview@spup.edu.ph with the review result and the Ethics Compliance Matrix Form. Choose Approve (which means RECEIVED).
  6. Download the files and put them together in a folder. Accomplish the Ethics Compliance Matrix Form and the revisions of the document as per recommendation.
  7. Send an APPROVAL REQUEST to cprint_ethicsreview@spup.edu.ph with the zipped folder attached. You will receive an approval notification. Choose Approve (which means RECEIVED).
  8. You will receive an approval request from cprint_ethicsreview@spup.edu.ph with the Certification of Approval. Choose Approve which means RECEIVED).
